Bars of the Nothern Quarter

Although the Northern Quarter houses plenty of different style bars, below you will find two of our choices which are worth noting. The Whisky Jar and Kosmonaut are both found on Tariff Street and both bars have their own originality. The Whisky Jar, is famous for its huge display of whiskies, with a piano that any pianist can sit down and play, very out west style bar. While Kosmonaut with its large spaces is great for a night out starter, a selection of beers and wines from any of the three bars inside and table tennis underneath the main room.

The Whisky Jar


The Whisky Jar is perfect for that old traditional feel. With plenty of seating arrangement and friendly staff on service, this place has a great atmoshphere with lots of selections of drinks on offer. The prices are a little more expensive than the average drink, but its well worth a night out or to take a date.

Kosmonaut

Kosmonaut has it all, with two floors and three rooms including three bars, theres plenty to choose from. With ample space and plenty of tables and chairs, you wont feel cramped here. Wether you want to chill with a few beers with some friends, or pop downstairs and compete with a game of table tennis, this place is a must go for a great start to any night.

Go and enjoy!
